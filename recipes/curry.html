Curry Recipe

Description

So, here we will be providing the steps to make famous Indian aloo-curry

Ingredients used

  • 3to4 medium Potato (peeled and cubed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomato (chopped or pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • ½ tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • ½ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • ½ tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 to 1.5 cups water
  • Fresh Coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and Cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and the oil separates.
  5. Mix turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  6. Add the potato cubes and mix well with the masala.
  7. Pour in water, cover, and cook for 15 to 20 minutes until potatoes are soft.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
