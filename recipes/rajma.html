Rajma Recipe

Description

So, here we will be providing the steps to make Rajma dish

Ingredients used

  • 1 cup soaked Rajma
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomato (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or Butter
  • ½ tsp Cumin Seeds
  • ½ tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • ½ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak rajma overnight, then pressure cook until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and Cook for about 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  6. Add cooked rajma with some of its cooking water.
  7. Cook on low heat for 15 to 20 minutes so the gravy thickens.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.

Now you can serve this hot with rice or brread.
