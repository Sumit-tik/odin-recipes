Pasta Recipe

Description

So, here we will be providing the steps to make Creamy White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients used

  • 2 cups Pasta (penne or macaroni)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 cups Milk
  • ½ cup Cheese (grated)
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (like Bell Pepper, Sweet Corn, Carrot)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water for 8 to 10 minutes until soft. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat a pan and lightly sauté the vegetables for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Make white sauce by melting butter in a pan.
  4. flour and cook for 1 minute while stirring.
  5. slowly pour in milk and whisk to avoid lumps.
  6. Add cheese, salt, and black pepper. Cook until the sauce becomes creamy.
  7. Mix turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  8. Add the boiled pasta and vegetables to the sauce and mix well.
  9. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve hot.
